ThreadBound Privacy Policy

Effective date: 17 May 2026 · Applies to the ThreadBound Android application

ThreadBound is a 100% offline tool for backing up and exporting your own SMS, MMS, and RCS conversations. This policy explains exactly what ThreadBound does and does not do with your data. It mirrors, verbatim, the “Help & Privacy” information shown inside the app.

This app uses READ_SMS and READ_CONTACTS for user content backup, restore, and local archive only.

How ThreadBound works

ThreadBound reads your SMS, MMS, and RCS messages directly from your device — the same source your messaging app uses. Nothing is sent to any server. Exports run entirely offline. Files land in your Downloads folder; you choose where they go from there.

ThreadBound cannot delete, edit, or send messages. It is a read-only tool.

The first time ThreadBound runs, it asks for permission to read your messages and contacts. Contact access is optional — without it, ThreadBound shows phone numbers instead of names.

Privacy & your data

ThreadBound stores a copy of your conversations only when you choose to archive them. Archived data is encrypted on your device using Android’s secure storage. It is not backed up to any cloud service.

ThreadBound collects no analytics, sends no crash reports, and makes no network calls of any kind. There is no account, no sync, and no way for ThreadBound to access your data remotely.

Note on fonts: ThreadBound uses standard fonts (Crimson Pro, Inter, JetBrains Mono). On a fresh install, your device may briefly download these fonts from Google Fonts via Play Services — this is a one-time system-level fetch, not a ThreadBound network call. After the first install, fonts are cached locally.

Legal export caveats: Structured-log PDFs are designed to meet FRE 901/902 requirements, but ThreadBound cannot guarantee admissibility. Consult legal counsel for evidentiary use.

Data we collect

None. ThreadBound does not collect, transmit, or share any data with the developer or any third party. The READ_SMS and READ_CONTACTS permissions are used exclusively on your device, at your explicit direction, to display and export your own content. No message content, contact data, metadata, or derived data ever leaves your device.

Data sharing

ThreadBound does not share data with third parties. Export files (PDF, ZIP) are created on your device and are shared only through Android’s system share sheet when you explicitly choose to share them. ThreadBound performs no automatic sharing and no cloud upload.

Data retention and deletion

Archived data is stored only in ThreadBound’s private storage on your device. To delete all ThreadBound data, uninstall the app: Android removes all of the app’s private files, archives, encrypted database, cached media, and secure-storage keys at uninstall, with no residue and no cloud copy.

Security

Archived conversations are encrypted at rest on your device using a key held in Android’s hardware-backed secure storage. ThreadBound does not transmit data, so there is no data in transit to protect.

Children’s privacy

ThreadBound is a general-utility backup tool intended for adults and is not directed to children. ThreadBound collects no data from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy
